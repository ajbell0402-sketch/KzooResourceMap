    About Kzoo Resources

    This site is a community project aimed at making it easier for Kalamazoo-area residents to find free and low-cost food, clothing, and essential resources — all in one place.

    Know of a resource that's missing? Have updated hours or contact info? Get in touch — this site is only as good as the community that maintains it.

    What's listed here

    🥫 Food Pantries & Banks
    Fixed-location pantries and food banks with regular distribution hours.
    👕 Free Stores
    Locations where you can take clothing, household goods, and more at no cost.
    🚐 Mobile Distribution
    Mobile food trucks and pop-up markets that visit different neighborhoods.
    📅 Coming soon
    Low-barrier healthcare clinics, ESL classes, free youth events, and more.

    Data & calendar

    Event dates are maintained in a Google Calendar and updated as new information becomes available. Hours and dates change — always call ahead or check the organization's website before visiting.

    For organizations

    If you run a food pantry, free store, or community resource in the Kalamazoo area and want to be listed here, please reach out. This directory is free and non-commercial.

    Geographic coverage

    This site covers Kalamazoo, Portage, Comstock, Parchment, Plainwell, and surrounding communities in Kalamazoo County and the broader Southwest Michigan region.
